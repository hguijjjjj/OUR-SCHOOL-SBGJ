    • SYMPHONY




    THE SYMPHONY OF JOY Sarala Birla Gyan Jyoti celebrated the Cultural Extravaganza, SYMPHONY '22, with unprecedented fanfare on 13th August 2022, at the school premises. The talented students presented a mesmerizing show that won the hearts of everyone. Dignitaries from different fields such as Education, Media, Health, and Creative Arts attended the programme. Students in large number came forward to showcase their potent talents to create a positive environment. They enthralled the audience with their amazing skills and talent that came to light and conveyed the message of happiness and wellbeing to all. The programme concluded with the members of the SBGJ, Guwahati family, expressing gratitude to the Principal, Dr. Diganta Halder for his expert guidance and leadership in leading Sarala Birla Gyan Jyoti, Guwahati to great heights.
